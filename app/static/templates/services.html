Services

CLDC offers a wide array of reliable services for Howard Faculty, Graduate, and Undergraduate Students

CLDC User Accounts

CLDC offers free user accounts to all faculty members and validated students in the School of Engineering, Architecture and Computer Science. These accounts give users access to all available applications in CLIDE room 2104.

Guidlines for Choosing Passwords
  1. Do not use your own name.
  2. Do not use your mother's, father's, sister's, or anybody's name.
  3. Do not make it a dictionary word.
  4. The password should be 6-8 characters.
  5. The password should include:
    • Numbers
    • Upper and lower case combinations.

Internet Access

The CLDC lab offers its users constant access to the internet. The world wide web is just a click away for all its users.

Wireless

CLDC provides a wireless internet connection service which enables users to connect to the internet using their laptops in and around the engineering library and Room 2110 (AKA, the Purple Room, or CCReV).

Plotter Printing Services

The plotter is available for printing at the following costs:

  • Black & White, plain paper: $1/sq ft
  • Color, plain paper: $5/sq ft
  • Color, glossy paper: $8/sq/ft.
Please see the nearest operator/administrator on duty to acquire the request form/receipt.
